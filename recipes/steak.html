Steak Recipe

Description:

The recipe is a very straight forward, quick recipe. Perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients:

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Cooking Oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Season steak as needed
  2. Sear steak on one side for 3.5 minutes
  3. Flip steak and add garlic, rosemary, and thyme
  4. Add butter and baste until cooked
  5. Let rest for 10 minutes before cutting in
